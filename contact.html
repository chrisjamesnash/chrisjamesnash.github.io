Contact

To get in touch, please email georgina@georginaskinner.co.uk

Georgina is available to perform at a range of formal or informal events such as weddings, dinners and parties. She can sing from a range of soprano repertoire including opera, musical theatre and classical songs, and will choose appropriate music to suit any occasion. She may be able to accommodate requests for specific songs, and she may also be able to bring a pianist to accompany her. Recent events include:

  • Opera night - The Hop and Huffkin, Sandwich
  • May ball - Peterhouse College, Cambridge
  • May ball - Trinity College, Cambridge
  • May ball - Jesus College, Cambridge
  • Italian evening - Sandwich Church Hall
  • Winter Ball - Selwyn College, Cambridge
  • Winter Ball - Fitzwilliam College, Cambridge
  • Restaraunt opening - The Hop and Huffkin, Sandwich
  • Summer concert - The Salutation, Sandwich

Cambridge University graduate student and classically trained soprano specialising in musical theatre and opera
